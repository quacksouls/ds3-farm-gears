Rusted (Gold) Coin

Rusted (Gold) Coin

These coins allow you to temporarily boost your Item Discovery by at least 50 points for 60 seconds. When you are farming for items, popping a Rusted Coin would increase your Item Discovery by 50 points, an effect that lasts for 60 seconds. Popping a Rusted Gold Coin would boost your Item Discovery by 100 points for 60 seconds. The Undead Settlement is the earliest part of the game where you are able to obtain 2 Rusted Coins. To purchase an unlimited number of Rusted Coins, consider doing Patches' quest line. When you create your character before you start the game, you have a choice of 7 Rusted Gold Coins as your burial gift. There are a limited number of Rusted Gold Coins that can be obtained throughout the game. However, the Rusted (Gold) Coins can be farmed from the Jailer Handmaids in the Profaned Capital.
